SERVICES

Disability Support and Care

We are committed to providing personalized care to individuals with disabilities. Our experienced medical professionals create customized care plans to help clients reach their goals and improve their quality of life in a comfortable and supportive environment.

Rehabilitation

We offer custom rehabilitation plans to meet each client's unique needs. Our professionals provide physical, occupational, and speech therapy, as well as mental health counseling.

Respite Care

At ATHC, we offer personalized respite care services that cater to the unique needs of each caregiver and care recipient. Our support includes compassionate care, personal assistance, and transportation services to help you through challenging times.

Medication Management

We offer medication management to improve patient health outcomes by ensuring appropriate medication use. This includes medication reconciliation, therapy management, and patient education.

Care Companion

We offer customized care companion services to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our staff is highly trained to provide physical and emotional support, and we strive to create a sense of community. Trust us to take good care of your loved ones.

Home Assistance

Our home assistance service helps clients maintain their independence, privacy, and dignity by providing household management, personal care, childcare, and companionship services. We work with clients to create customized plan that meets their needs and helps them achieve their goals.

Household Management

Our Household Management service simplifies household chores. We offer cleaning, laundry, grocery shopping, meal preparation, and organization services. Our experienced professionals help you declutter and maximize your living space. With our assistance, you can focus your time and energy on what matters most to you.

Personal Care

Our service offers assistance with everyday tasks such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting. Additionally, our caregivers can help with medication reminders, meal prep, and light housekeeping, so you can live safely and comfortably in your own home.

Childcare

Our Childcare services are delivered by caring professionals who work closely with parents to create a nurturing environment that promotes learning and development. We offer a variety of engaging activities, including arts and crafts, outdoor play, and music lessons, that cater to the unique interests of each child. Nutritious meals are also provided.

Mental Disability

We offer a variety of services to people with mental disabilities. Our team of professionals provides personalized treatment plans, therapy sessions, medication management, and support for daily activities. We also host recreational activities and social events to promote socialization and community integration. It is our belief that every individual deserves a fulfilling life, and we are committed to helping them achieve their goals.
