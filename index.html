Mission

Our twenty-five years of experience in health care services enables us to employ highly qualified and experienced professionals with a medical background to properly understand our client's needs and requirements. It is our commitment and responsibility to understand the importance of each client's independence, privacy, confidentiality, and respect for the client's input and point of view, regardless of ethnicity and beliefs, in addition to understanding our client's physical and mental well -being via easy communication skills. We aim to establish with our clients a personal protocol to work together until our mutual goal is achieved via the application of full transparency, commitment, and honesty throughout the treatment cycle. Having our head office in Rotorua reassure Rotorua clients' confidence that they will be highly looked after.

Vision

Advanced Total Healthcare Limited believes staying healthy is a pivotal element in our livelihood. We are pleased to choose Rotorua to host our firm head office. Why Rotorua? Rotorua is rich in history and diverse natural beauty, which is noticeably reflected in its citizens. Twenty-Five years of experience in healthcare in overseas services and in New Zealand give us a better understanding of clients' requirements and needs. Advanced Total Health Care focuses on a specific clientele base that requires special needs, which enables us to offer outstanding healthcare services. Our aim is not to have a database that we cannot handle and eventually compromise our services. Rather, we aim to provide each client with a hassle-free treatment cycle through a set-tailored protocol that ensures each and every cycle is completed on time.
