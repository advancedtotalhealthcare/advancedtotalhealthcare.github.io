Mission

Our twenty-five years of experience in health care services enables us to employ highly qualified and experienced professionals with a medical background to properly understand our client's needs and requirements. It is our commitment and responsibility to understand the importance of each client's independence, privacy, confidentiality, and respect for the client's input and point of view, regardless of ethnicity and beliefs, in addition to understanding our client's physical and mental well -being via easy communication skills. We aim to establish with our clients a personal protocol to work together until our mutual goal is achieved via the application of full transparency, commitment, and honesty throughout the treatment cycle. Having our head office in Rotorua reassure Rotorua clients' confidence that they will be highly looked after.

Services

Disability Support and Care

The goal of disability support and care is to enhance the quality of life and promote independence for individuals with disabilities.

Rehabilitation

The ultimate goal of healthcare rehabilitation is to help patients return to their normal daily activities as much as possible.

Respite Care

The primary goal of healthcare respite care is to improve the quality of life for both the individual receiving care and their caregiver by allowing them to rest and recharge.

Medication Management

The goal of healthcare medication management is to ensure that patients are receiving safe and effective treatment, and to minimize the risk of medication-related problems such as adverse drug reactions, medication errors, and non-adherence.

Care Companion

The goal of a healthcare care companion is to improve the overall quality of life for patients by providing compassionate care and support.

Home Assistance

The goal of home assistance is to allow individuals to remain in their own homes as long as possible, while maintaining their independence and quality of life.

Household Management

The goal of healthcare household management is to help individuals maintain a clean and safe living environment, while promoting their independence and quality of life.

Personal Care

The goal of healthcare personal care is to help individuals maintain their personal hygiene and grooming, and promote their physical comfort and well-being.

Childcare

The goal of healthcare childcare is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for children, while allowing parents and guardians to focus on their medical care.

Mental Disability

The goal of healthcare for individuals with mental disabilities is to help them achieve their full potential and lead fulfilling lives, while managing the symptoms of their condition.
